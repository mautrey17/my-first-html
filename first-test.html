How to make a sandwich

  1. Locate all ingredients necessary: Bread, ham, cheese, mustard
  2. Carry all ingredients to crafting station and place on station
  3. Open bag of bread, put hand inside bag, pull out 2 pieces and place on the plate. Put both pieces of bread with white side facing up side by side on the plate. CLose the bag of bread
  4. Open bag of ham. Put hand inside, grab 2 pieces of ham, take ham out, put on one piece of bread.
